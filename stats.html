Artwork Artist's Commentary
Make Some Noise
Made in April of 2025, this piece was originally just a
simple sketch for a JOUR 101 assignment before I
decided to make it a complete piece.
Can I Call You a
Dancin' Master?
 Made in July of 2025, the piece was made for an
event called ArtFight, an art gifting game. The piece
uses aesthetics from Dance Dance Revolution
Supernova 2. The piece is currently on display at the
Donna Beam Art Gallery as part of their exhibit.
War Never Ends
 Made in December of 2025, this piece was made as
part of a Secret Santa event. The piece takes visual
inspiration from the Metal Slug anthology of games.
On release, it came with chibi designs of War Dog
and Hops, the characters featured in the piece.
Logo Design Infographic
 Made in October of 2025, the infographic takes the
reader to a brief rundown about Logo Design and
what makes a good logo work. This was used for a
GRC 320 Assignment
Toy Car Package
 Made in April of 2025, this shows a layout for a toy
car package. I based it off a Crysler Prowler toy I
own. The piece was used in a GRC 250 Assignment.
DDR Arcade Screen
Border Recreation
 Made in February of 2026, the piece is a recreation
of a Dance Dance Revolution screen border. I made
it out of necessity, as existing replicas were too low
quality for my needs. This was used as a frame for
"Can I Call You a Dancin' Master?" which can now be
seen on display at the Donna Beam Art Gallery.
